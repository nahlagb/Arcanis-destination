Tourist attractions

  • Nature Park: Enjoy the beautiful nature of Arcanis in this wonderful nature park. Here u can have an unpolluted haven full of animals and plants including extinct ones, perfect for nature lovers. Ever wondered how it is like talking to animals, well you will find out in this nature park.
  • Adventure Park: Love the thrill of adventure and enjoy the endless possibilities. Go for adventurous rafting, bungee jumping, cave and forest exploration, etc. All the things that will make your heart race. This adventure park is capable of turning the most laidback individuals into adventure lovers.
  • Amusement Park: We have stuff for the smaller ones too, with fun amusement rides and even a roller coaster so high that you can see the whole of space. Ever heard of Art in the air, Arcanis enables you to write or draw in the air, exhaust your creative imagination here.
